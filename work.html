How I work

Every project is unique

Each project's specific challenges require a custom process. The general steps to solve problems remain however similar.

  • Define the problem, usually with stakeholders
  • Conduct research to understand the users
  • Match business goals with user needs
  • Collaborative Ideation phase
  • Iterative wireframing, prototyping and testing
  • Making sure the solution is well implemented
  • Tracking the outcome and optimizing continuously

Each step can involve diverse research and design methods, but can be efficiently presented as a limited set of deliverables.

Defining the problem

I usually define the process with stakeholders based on the goals, current state, time frame and budget of the project to give you the best value for your money.

Understand the user through personas

Personas are a synthetic presentation of insights about users uncovered through user research. It helps share a common, realistic vision of users across stakeholders and is a useful tool for ideations and iteration reviews.
